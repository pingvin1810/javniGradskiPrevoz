IDEA

The essence of the project is public transport in the city of Sarajevo. The idea is to show in a simple way the routes of all types of traffic as well as their driving schedule, so that each user can find the necessary information for themselves.
We saw the need for a site like this. The user is our number one priority and his needs for information.
Everyone has a need for transport, why shouldn't such information be available to a user. Available in a few clicks!
